Cabayanos Island

Cabayanos is inspired of Tenedosisland

Cabayanos (16.3 km2) is smaller than Tenedos (42.63 km2). While the sattelite map, elevation and roads are based on the real thing, I've taken a lot of liberties to produce some interesting locations for Arma3.

Why another island for arma 3 ?

Well, i've got an other project on the road to create a Multiplayer & Single player campaign for this island
